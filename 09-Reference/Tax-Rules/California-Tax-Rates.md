## Key California Tax Items

### LLC Annual Tax and Fee
- **Minimum franchise tax**: $800 per year (due April 15)
- **LLC fee** (based on total income from CA sources):

| Total Income | Fee |
|-------------|-----|
| $250,000 - $499,999 | $900 |
| $500,000 - $999,999 | $2,500 |
| $1,000,000 - $4,999,999 | $6,000 |
| $5,000,000+ | $11,790 |

### California-Specific Notes
- CA does NOT conform to all federal deductions
- CA standard deduction is much lower than federal (~$5,540 single / ~$11,080 MFJ)
- CA does NOT allow SALT deduction (already included in state tax)
- CA conforms to federal treatment of QBI deduction with modifications
- CA taxes capital gains as ordinary income (no preferential rate)
- Mental Health Services Tax: additional 1% on taxable income over $1,000,000

### Estimated Tax Payments
- Same quarterly schedule as federal (April 15, June 15, September 15, January 15)
- Use Form 540-ES
- Safe harbor: 100% of prior year tax (110% if CA AGI > $150,000) or 90% of current year

### Filing Deadlines
- April 15: Return due (or automatic 6-month extension to October 15)
- CA grants automatic extension to file (not to pay)
