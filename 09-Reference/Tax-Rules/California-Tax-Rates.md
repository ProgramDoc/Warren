# California State Tax — 2026 (Official)

## Income Tax Brackets (Single Filer)

| Taxable Income | Rate |
|---------------|------|
| $0 - $11,079 | 1% |
| $11,080 - $26,264 | 2% |
| $26,265 - $41,867 | 4% |
| $41,868 - $52,455 | 6% |
| $52,456 - $66,295 | 8% |
| $66,296 - $371,479 | 9.3% |
| $371,480 - $445,771 | 10.3% |
| $445,772 - $742,953 | 11.3% |
| $742,954 - $999,999 | 12.3% |
| Over $1,000,000 | 13.3% (includes 1% Mental Health Services Tax) |

## Income Tax Brackets (Married Filing Jointly)

Thresholds are generally double the single filer amounts. Top rate of 13.3% applies to income over $2,000,000 (MFJ).

## California Standard Deduction (2026 Official)

| Filing Status | Amount |
|--------------|--------|
| Single / MFS | $5,706 |
| MFJ / Head of Household | $11,412 |

Note: California's standard deduction is much smaller than the federal standard deduction. Most taxpayers with significant income itemize on their CA return.

## LLC Annual Tax and Fees (2026 Official)

### Annual Franchise Tax
- **Minimum**: $800 per year (due April 15)
- **First-year exemption**: LLCs formed on or after January 1, 2024 are exempt for their first taxable year

### LLC Fee (based on total California-source gross income)

| Total Income | Fee |
|-------------|-----|
| Under $250,000 | $0 |
| $250,000 - $499,999 | $900 |
| $500,000 - $999,999 | $2,500 |
| $1,000,000 - $4,999,999 | $6,000 |
| $5,000,000+ | $11,790 |

### Other LLC Filing Requirements
- **Articles of Organization**: $70 filing fee
- **Statement of Information**: $20, due within 90 days of formation, then biennially
- **S-Corp tax** (if S-Corp election): 1.5% of net income (minimum $800)

## California-Specific Tax Notes

- CA does NOT conform to all federal deductions
- CA does NOT allow SALT deduction (you're already paying state tax)
- CA taxes capital gains as **ordinary income** (no preferential rate)
- CA conforms to federal QBI deduction with modifications
- Mental Health Services Tax: additional 1% on taxable income over $1,000,000
- CA grants automatic 6-month extension to **file** (not to pay)

## Estimated Tax Payments
