## Key Thresholds

- **Net Investment Income Tax (NIIT)**: 3.8% on net investment income for AGI > $200K (single) / $250K (MFJ)
- **Additional Medicare Tax**: 0.9% on wages/SE income > $200K (single) / $250K (MFJ)
- **SALT Deduction Cap**: $10,000 (state and local taxes, if itemizing)
- **Qualified Business Income (QBI) Deduction**: 20% of qualified business income, subject to limitations
- **AMT Exemption**: ~$85,700 (single) / ~$133,300 (MFJ), phaseout at ~$609,350 / ~$1,218,700

## Self-Employment Tax (2026 Official)

- **Social Security**: 12.4% on first **$184,500** of SE income (up from $176,100 in 2025)
- **Medicare**: 2.9% on all SE income (no cap)
- **Additional Medicare**: 0.9% on SE income over $200K (single) / $250K (MFJ)
- **Calculation base**: 92.35% of net SE income
- **Above-the-line deduction**: 50% of SE tax is deductible

## Estimated Tax Penalty Avoidance

Pay at least the lesser of:
- 90% of current year tax liability, OR
- 100% of prior year tax liability (110% if prior year AGI > $150,000)

through withholding + estimated payments to avoid underpayment penalty.
