# Warren Financial Reports

Reports turn raw financial data into decisions. Warren generates structured summaries at multiple cadences so Tom always knows where he stands.

## Year-End Review

Generated in January for the prior year. Stored in `08-Reports/Annual/`:

Comprehensive annual summary including:
- Full year income by source with month-by-month breakdown
- Full year expenses by category with budget comparison
- Net worth change over the year
- Tax filing readiness assessment
- Investment performance summary (when Investment module is active)
- Goal progress review
- Recommendations for the new year

## Alfred Integration Summary

Warren generates a daily financial intelligence briefing for Alfred:

```
WARREN DAILY BRIEF — [date]

URGENT:
- [Tax deadline in 3 days / Bill overdue / Budget alert]

STATUS:
- Cash position: $X across liquid accounts
- MTD spending: $X of $X budget (X%)

FYI:
- [Upcoming payment / Expected income / Trend note]
```

This is stored in the most recent daily ledger entry and structured so Alfred can parse and include it in the morning briefing.
